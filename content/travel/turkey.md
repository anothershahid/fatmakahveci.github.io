### The Best Places for Breakfast-Lunch-Dinner

- [Demirkollar Ekmek Fırını](https://goo.gl/maps/JYJDnxjD8qaAJjWy6) Nokul with minced meat or with walnut and raisin (Kıymalı / Cevizli üzümlü nokul)
- [Şen Pastanesi](https://goo.gl/maps/pmHJ87qJgTxXusDB8) Ice Cream and Semolina cake soaked in light syrup (Dondurma ve Revani)
- [Okyanus Balıkevi](https://goo.gl/maps/47VgTeGoN7MhcNr5A) Seafood (Deniz ürünleri)
- [Şekerci Mehmet Gürbüz](https://goo.gl/maps/kKqBxsya1zPDvGpW7) Walnut paste (Boyabat ezmesi)
- [Körfez 2 Lokantası](https://goo.gl/maps/fU27WyqmEdvGP4wd6) Turkish pizza with egg and minced meat (Yumurtalı kıymalı pide)
- [Teyze'nin Yeri Mantı Salonu](https://goo.gl/maps/KNu2Gq6ndFyaWyuQ6) Turkish ravioli with minced meat served with walnut, yoghurt, butter and tomato sauce (Cevizli ve yoğurtlu mantı)
- [Kahveci baba](https://www.google.com/maps/place/Kahveci+baba/@42.027042,35.1457043,17z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m5!3m4!1s0x408f133df6346765:0xd6a118adc4f3e71b!8m2!3d42.027042!4d35.147893) Turkish coffee (Türk kahvesi)
